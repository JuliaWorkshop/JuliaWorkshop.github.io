Rows and Columns
With a 2D array, you use brackets, colons, and commas to extract individual rows and columns or ranges of rows and columns. With this table: 

julia> table = [r * c for r in 1:5, c in 1:5]
5x5 Array{Int64,2}:
1   2   3   4   5
2   4   6   8  10
3   6   9  12  15
4   8  12  16  20
5  10  15  20  25
you can find a single row using the following (notice the comma): 

julia> table[1,:]
1x5 Array{Int64,2}:
1  2  3  4  5
and you can get a range of rows with a range followed by a comma and a colon: 

julia> table[2:3,:]
2x5 Array{Int64,2}:
2  4  6   8  10
3  6  9  12  15
For columns, start with a colon followed by a comma: 

julia> table[:,2]
5-element Array{Int64,1}:
 2
 4
 6
 8
10

julia> table[:,2:3]
